Our services

We are here to meet all your needs

Free Hearing Check

Our expert team offers a free assessment of your hearing sensitivity through tonal and speech audiometric tests in a completely silent booth for a better understanding of your hearing.

Hearing Aid Information

At our affiliated pharmacies, we offer the latest generation of digital hearing aids. Our commitment is to help you choose the device that best suits your needs.

Assisted Sales and Warranty

We offer a 24-month warranty on all our hearing aids, covering manufacturing defects. This service guarantees complete peace of mind and improved product quality.

Programming and Assistance

Hearing aid programming and cleaning are free services. Our hearing care professional will help you schedule regular appointments to monitor the progress and proper functioning of your hearing.

Hearing Aid Fitting & Customization

After your hearing assessment, we offer personalized fitting and fine-tuning of hearing aids based on your audiogram and lifestyle needs. This ensures comfort and optimal hearing performance tailored just for you.

Follow-Up Care & Audiological Guidance

Our team schedules regular follow-up visits to monitor your progress, adjust settings if needed, and provide ongoing support and advice to help you get the most from your hearing solutions.

Chi Siamo

M.A.X.O. Audioprotesi è specializzata nel migliorare la qualità dell’udito delle persone grazie a soluzioni audioprotesiche moderne, discrete e tecnologicamente avanzate.

Utilizziamo componentistica digitale miniaturizzata per offrire protesi acustiche che si distinguono per facilità d’uso, comfort ed elevata qualità sonora, ideali per ascoltare con naturalezza in ogni situazione.

Il nostro impegno è accompagnarti passo dopo passo nel tuo percorso uditivo, dalla valutazione gratuita dell’udito alla scelta personalizzata del dispositivo, fino ai servizi di programmazione, assistenza e follow‑up. La nostra equipe di esperti è sempre al tuo fianco per garantire supporto professionale e attenzione alle tue esigenze uniche.
